Bike Shop is the biggest manufacturer and supplier of modern high-tech bicycles, spare parts and accessories in Ukraine. Our company was founded in 1999. All of the Bike Shop bicycles are assembling on a modern conveyor line in Kyiv, Ukraine.

Among our partners there are well-known companies all over the world, such as SHIMANO, SUNTOUR, ALEXRIMS, ARTEK, BENGAL, PROWHEEL, QUANDO HUBS, STANDWELL, KMC CHAIN, ZOOM, CHENG SHIN TIRE, VELO, NECO and many other producers of bicycle spare parts. Wheel assembly is carried out on equipment of Dutch company HOLLAND MECHANICS which is an expert in manufacture of equipment for the bicycles production. Each wheel undergoes computer balancing, which eliminates the horizontal and vertical wobbling, that means the wheel becomes perfectly even.

Our company assembles a wide range of bicycles models that gives us more opportunities and ways to meet needs and tastes of our customers.

Work is constantly under way to improve the quality and design of products. Highly qualified specialists carry out all processes, starting from developing drawings to ready products release.
